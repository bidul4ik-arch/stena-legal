Copyright Policy

STENA respects intellectual property rights.

1. Reporting Infringement

If you believe content on STENA infringes your copyright, send a notice to:

Email: admin@dkleading.com

Your notice must include:

  • Description of copyrighted work
  • Location of infringing content
  • Your contact information
  • Statement of good faith belief
  • Statement under penalty of perjury

2. Counter-Notice

Users may submit a counter-notice if they believe content was removed in error.

3. Repeat Infringers

Accounts repeatedly violating copyright may be terminated.
