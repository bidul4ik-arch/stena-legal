Community Guidelines

STENA is a platform for communication and expression. To maintain a safe and respectful environment, users must comply with the following rules:

1. Respect Others

  • No harassment or bullying
  • No hate speech or discrimination
  • No threats or incitement to violence

2. Illegal Content

  • No illegal activities
  • No promotion of criminal behavior
  • No distribution of malware

3. Explicit Content

  • No sexual exploitation
  • No explicit content involving minors

4. Impersonation

Do not impersonate other individuals or organizations.

5. Enforcement

Violations may result in content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination.

6. Reporting

Users may report violations by contacting: admin@dkleading.com
