Child Safety Standards

STENA is intended for users aged 13 years and older.

Age Restriction

The platform is not designed for children under 13. Users must confirm they meet the minimum age requirement before accessing the Service.

Protection Measures

  • Community Guidelines prohibit harassment, exploitation, and harmful content.
  • Users can report inappropriate content or behavior.
  • Accounts violating safety rules may be suspended or permanently removed.
  • We do not tolerate child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or exploitation.

Reporting

Users may report violations through in-app reporting tools or by contacting:

Email: admin@dkleading.com

Content Moderation

Reported content is reviewed and appropriate action is taken, including removal of content and account suspension where necessary.
